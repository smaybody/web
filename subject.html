Jazz piano solo styles

  • Slow american jazz
    • You can play the melody and chords softly as written on sheet music
    • You can also play a bass note (or another chord tone) while comping or playing arpeggios on right hand
  • Fast american jazz
    • Walking bass, charleston, and stride all work well when played quickly
    • Stride is somehwat versatile; you can play it slowly and have it sound decent too
  • Latin jazz
    • Examples include samba, bossa nova, and montuno
    • All three of these styles are usually fast, and while they can all be played using a bossa nova bass line, montuno has its own distinct rhythm as well
