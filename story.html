Come Join Prince Theodore on His Quest!

Welcome to the tale of Prince Theodore, in which you can control our protagonist as he journeys throughout his former kingdom trying to make ends meet. On your travels you will face perils ranging from bar fights to duels with imperial soldiers, and either emerge victorious or die trying depending on what actions you choose to take.

About the Story

The story takes place in the kingdom of Grandworth, ruled by king Astor and his court. The high fantasy setting introduces you to a bustling market town furnished with shops, vendors, and cottages, along with thieves, assassins, and rowdy drunkards. During your journey, you will venture through a winding forest, fending for yourself as you make your way towards your goal.
